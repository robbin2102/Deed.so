A Note from the Founder

Why We're Pausing:

  • Onboarding creators from web2 to web3 on a new dapp has proven more challenging than expected
  • Retention economics is a key issue
  • Network effects not as strong in web3 for a new web2 creator

The Future Path:

Integrate with established web3 creator platforms like Zora or Farcaster and make Deed an onboarding layer, where with a click, any creator on web2 apps can import all their content, metrics onchain and launch tokens linked to the full set of intellectual property (content) that they built over web2 over many years.
