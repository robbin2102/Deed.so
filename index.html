A Note from the Founder

Why We're Pausing:

After months of building and testing Deed on Solana testnet, we've made the difficult decision to pause the project. This decision comes after deep reflection on market timing, user adoption patterns, and the challenges of bridging Web2 and Web3 economies.

What We Learned:

The problem Deed set out to solve remains incredibly relevant: 200M+ online communities still lack native ways to earn from asset growth, contribute to development, or share in revenues. Our testnet validated the technical feasibility with $2.8M+ in trade volume and meaningful community engagement.

The Vision Still Matters:

We still believe in a future where communities don't just consume, but co-create, contribute, and capture value from the assets they love. The infrastructure for onchain economies and capital markets for internet assets will be critical as Web3 matures.
